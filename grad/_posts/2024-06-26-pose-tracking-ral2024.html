--- title: Certifiable Category-Level Tracking description: My first publication with SPARK Lab considers the problem of simultaneously estimating an object's shape and tracking its pose. We propose a certifiably optimal method built on the convex relaxation of a fixed lag smoother. We show the method largely outperforms others on simulated data, the YCBInEOAT manipulation dataset, and a custom drone vehicle tracking scenario.
