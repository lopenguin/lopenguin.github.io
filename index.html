Lorenzo Shaikewitz

I am an incoming graduate student at MIT AeroAstro advised by Prof. Luca Carlone. Though my PhD is just beginning, I'm broadly interested in bridging the gap between navigation and interaction, enabling robots to work in human spaces.

Previously, I was at Caltech (Mechanical Engineering & Controls, '23) advised by Prof. Aaron Ames and Prof. Günter Niemeyer. My undergraduate projects included a compliant ankle exoskeleton (with Maegan Tucker & Prof. Ames), an in-mouth feeding robot (with Prof. Dorsa Sadigh at Stanford), and a piano playing robot for ME 134 (with Prof. Niemeyer).
